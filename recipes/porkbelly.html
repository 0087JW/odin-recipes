Cripsy Pork Belly

Pork Belly

Pork as dinner or snack

Pork belly is a the underside of a pig similar to bacon but with more fat than skin or meat.

Ingredients

Porky belly (skin on), 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 1 tsp peprika or favorite spice (optional), 1 tbsp white vinegar, 7 oz rock-salt.

Steps

  • Use fork or sharp skewer to poke many holes in the pork skin, avoid piercing the flesh.
  • Turn the pork belly skin side down, spread on olive oil, mix together and layer on salt-pepper-spice, include the sides.
  • Place pork skin side up and dry skin with paper towel, refrigerate for 12 hours.
    • Cook
  • Preheat oven to 350f or 180c.
  • Place pork onto large sheet of foil. Fold the sides of foil up around the pork to enclose it creating a bowl-like rim to contain the meat and salt.
  • Place pork in foil onto a baking tray. Brush skin with vinegar, then spread rock salt on the skin (avoid salt from spilling over).
  • Roast for 60 minutes - remove tray. Fold down foil and scrape off salt from pork skin and sides. Remove foil retrun pork belly to cooking tray.
  • Move upper most shelf to be 10" from heat source. Switch oven to broiler on med-high. Place tray on upper shelf for 20-25 minutes, rotating the tray once, until skin is crispy and puffed.
  • On cutting board, slice pork into 2" thick slices then into smaller slices. Serves well with mustard or sugar.
