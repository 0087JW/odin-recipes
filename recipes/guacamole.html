Fine guacamole

Smooth and Chunky Avocados

The recipe attatched to the link will make you fine guacamole in small portion. I will provide a detailed description to improve the dish with little effort, the following ingredients are required to create my favorite guacamole.

Ingredients

3 Large Avocados, 3oz. lime juice, 1 bushel cilantro, 1/4 cup cumin, 1 jalapeno, 1/2 red onion, garlic salt (as much as needed).

Steps

  • Pit and chop avocados in quarter size cubes.
  • Finely chop jalapeno, with or without seeds.
  • Dice cliantro.
  • Chop onion into quarter cubes or smaller.
  • Add all ingredients together into large bowl. Proceed to add garlic salt until acquired taste.
